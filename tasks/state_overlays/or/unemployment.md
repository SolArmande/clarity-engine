# TASK OVERLAY: Apply for unemployment (Oregon)

## REQUIREMENTS

- Add to baseline requirements: Access to Oregon’s Frances Online portal at https://frances.oregon.gov.
- Add to baseline requirements: WorkSource Oregon/iMatchSkills account information (required work registration unless you are exempt).

## STEPS

1. Replace baseline step 1 with: Open Oregon Employment Department’s unemployment portal at https://frances.oregon.gov.
2. Replace baseline steps 3–7 with: In Frances Online, select **Unemployment Insurance** and submit the **initial claim** flow.
3. Modify baseline step 23 to: Check your Frances Online message center for identity-proofing requests from Oregon.
4. Replace baseline step 24 with: Complete Oregon’s identity verification through ID.me if prompted, including identity document upload and selfie/live verification.
5. Add after baseline step 24: Complete WorkSource Oregon registration (iMatchSkills) within the deadline shown in your Oregon claim instructions unless your notice marks you exempt.
6. Replace baseline step 25 with: File an Oregon **weekly claim** in Frances Online for each prior claim week (Sunday through Saturday) as soon as the weekly filing window opens.
7. Add after baseline step 25: If you miss a weekly claim, submit a **restart claim** in Frances Online before filing the next weekly claim.

## DECISIONS

- IF Oregon sends an ID.me verification task in Frances → THEN complete ID.me before Oregon’s deadline or payment will be held.
- IF you are instructed to register for work search services → THEN complete WorkSource Oregon/iMatchSkills registration by the stated deadline.
- IF a week is missed and weekly claim filing is blocked → THEN file a restart claim first, then resume weekly claims.

## FAILURE POINTS

- Starting a claim on a non-Oregon site instead of Frances Online.
- Ignoring ID.me tasks in Frances messages.
- Missing the WorkSource Oregon/iMatchSkills registration deadline.
- Filing for the wrong claim week (Oregon weekly claims are for the prior Sunday–Saturday week).
- Trying to file another weekly claim without submitting a required restart claim after a missed week.
