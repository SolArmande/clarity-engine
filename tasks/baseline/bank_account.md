# TASK: Open a bank account (no prior banking)

## OUTCOME

You have an active checking account with online access and a funded balance.

## REQUIREMENTS

- Government-issued photo ID.
- Social Security number or ITIN.
- Proof of address.
- Initial deposit amount.
- Phone number and email address.
- Access to a bank branch or online application.

## STEPS

1. Choose 3 banks or credit unions in your area.
2. Compare monthly fees for basic checking accounts.
3. Compare minimum balance requirements.
4. Compare overdraft fee policies.
5. Compare ATM access and fees.
6. Select one account with the lowest total fees for your usage.
7. Start the application online or at a branch.
8. Enter your legal name and date of birth.
9. Enter your Social Security number or ITIN.
10. Enter your current address.
11. Upload or present photo ID.
12. Upload or present proof of address.
13. Review account terms and fee disclosures.
14. Agree to the account terms.
15. Fund the account with the initial deposit.
16. Create online banking username and password.
17. Enable two-factor authentication.
18. Install the bank mobile app.
19. Set up account alerts for low balance and transactions.
20. Confirm your debit card mailing address.
21. Activate your debit card when it arrives.
22. Set your debit card PIN.
23. Make one small purchase to confirm card works.

## DECISIONS

- IF you cannot meet minimum balance requirements → THEN choose an account with no minimum balance.
- IF you have no proof of address → THEN ask the bank for acceptable alternative documents.
- IF direct deposit is available from your employer → THEN request the bank direct deposit form and submit it to payroll.

## FAILURE POINTS

- Choosing an account without checking monthly fees.
- Missing required ID documents at application time.
- Not enabling transaction alerts.
- Forgetting to activate debit card.
- Triggering overdraft due to low balance.

## COMPLETION CHECK

- Account status is active.
- Initial deposit is posted.
- Online banking login works.
- Debit card is activated and tested.
- Alerts are enabled.
