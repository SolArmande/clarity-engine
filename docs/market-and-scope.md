# Market and Scope

## Market Landscape

High-friction public-service workflows are fragmented, form-heavy, and hard to complete under stress, especially on mobile.

## Problem Definition

Users fail tasks not because guidance is unavailable, but because guidance is not execution-ready.

## Chosen Wedge

**Public-service task completion** with step-by-step, atomic, completion-oriented workflows.

## Target Domains

- **Now:** UI-first delivery for core public-service task flows.
- **Near-term:** unemployment-related flows first.
- **Later (explicitly queued):** SNAP and adjacent public-benefit workflows.

## Non-Goals

- General chat assistant behavior.
- Long-form educational/policy explainers.
- Broad multi-domain expansion before proving execution success in the wedge.
